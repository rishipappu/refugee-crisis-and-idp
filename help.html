How Can You Help?

UNHCR

The UN Refugee Agency has put a multitude of humanitarian efforts into the issue of CAR. They provided refuge along with resources, awareness projects, articles, and aided the fight to a peaceful region. They have saved countless lives with community shelters and constant sustenance.

UNICEF

The UN Refugee Agency has put a multitude of humanitarian efforts into the issue of CAR. They provided refuge along with resources, awareness projects, articles, and aided the fight to a peaceful region. They have saved countless lives with community shelters and constant sustenance.

International Rescue Committee

The IRC, along with a spread of nutrition for the malnourished, clean water, sanitation aid, and sanctuary for young women and girls, has written multiple articles highlighting the issues that have become apparent in CAR along with warnings for women, girls, and children.

Additional Notes

Donations to all of these organizations can aid the fight against malnutrition and support protection in the region of the Central African Republic.
