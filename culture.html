Culture: Lifeblood of the Central African Republic

Even though the Central African Republic has developed a reputation as one of, if not the most, dangerously unstable nations in the world, the culture remains vibrant and diverse. The culture is very heavily inspired by their agricultural output, most prominently coffee and cotton; with local items like yams, corn, peanuts, and peppers also being added to the mix. Locals always eat with their hands, specifically the right hand. Shaking hands is a generally expected custom. Most locals dress modestly, even more so in Muslim areas.
